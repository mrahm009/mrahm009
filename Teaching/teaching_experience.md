# 🎓 Teaching Portfolio

## Teaching Philosophy Summary

> *"Every concept in biomedical engineering has a patient behind it. I teach with that consequence in mind."*

My teaching is built around three principles:
- **Meaning before method** — anchor technical content in clinical context before formalism
- **Productive struggle** — lab exercises designed around real experimental variability
- **Honest feedback** — rubric-based assessment with specific, actionable written feedback
